Research Overview

A Hybrid n-bit Multiplier Design on FPGA Leveraging Recursive-Wallace Tree for FIR Filters and Neural Network Inference

Publisher: IEEE 2023 International Conference on Next Generation Electronics (NEleX)

Focus: Optimization of n-bit multipliers using FPGA.

Approach: Hybrid AI-based method combining Wallace tree and Recursive algorithms.

Features:

  • Enhancements for FIR filters and neural network inference.
  • Unique cache management strategy using LRU (Least Recently Used) to handle hash collisions and reduce repeated calculations.

Results:

  • 49% reduction in delay compared to conventional multipliers.
  • 16% reduction in delay compared to Recursive Multipliers.
  • 13% increase in slack compared to Recursive and 59% compared to conventional multipliers.

Hardware Used: ALTERA DE2-115 FPGA

IoT-Based Smart Dam System

Publisher: IEEE 2023 Innovations in Power and Advanced Computing Technologies (i-PACT)

Focus: Smart dam system for water level management.

Approach: Integration of STM32F103C8T6 ARM Cortex microcontroller and ESP8266 with RS-485 serial communication.

Features:

  • Capacitive sensor for water level measurement.
  • Servo motor and infrared sensors for floodgate control.
  • Real-time monitoring via a website and notifications through IFTTT for emergencies.

Purpose: To manage water levels efficiently and prevent potential flooding.
